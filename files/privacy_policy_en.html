  • You means the individual accessing or using the App.

Data We Access

Apple HealthKit Workout Data

TriExport requests read-only access to Your multisport workouts in Apple HealthKit. This includes, for the workouts You choose to export:

  • Workout type, start time, and segment breakdown (swim, bike, run, transitions)
  • GPS route data recorded by Apple Watch
  • Heart rate, cadence, and cycling power samples

This data:

  • is read directly from HealthKit on Your Device,
  • is processed exclusively on Your Device to build GPX files,
  • is never transmitted to, accessed by, or stored on Our servers — We operate no servers in connection with the App.

You can review or revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → TriExport.

Generated GPX Files

The App writes GPX files to Your Device's temporary storage so You can share them via the iOS share sheet (for example, to upload them to Strava). What happens to a file after You share it is governed by the destination service's own privacy policy, not Ours.

Data We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any account information
  • Location data outside of the GPS samples already stored in Your HealthKit workouts (which never leave Your Device through the App)
  • Analytics, telemetry, or usage statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Payment information
  • Device contacts, photos, or camera data
  • Any other Personal Data

TriExport contains no third-party SDKs, no advertising frameworks, and no network calls of its own.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland: because all processing of Your workout data occurs locally on Your Device and We never receive or store any Personal Data, no controller-side processing under the GDPR takes place. Apple acts as the data controller for HealthKit data on Your Device in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If You are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, You have the following rights regarding any Personal Data We hold:

  • Right of access — You can request confirmation of whether We process Your Personal Data and obtain a copy.
  • Right to rectification — You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — You can request deletion of Your Personal Data.
  • Right to restriction — You can request that We restrict processing of Your data.
  • Right to data portability — You can request Your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent, You may withdraw it at any time.

Because We hold no Personal Data about You at all, most of the above rights have no practical content with respect to Us. You can manage Your HealthKit data through Apple's Health app, and revoke the App's HealthKit access at any time in Your Device settings.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In the Czech Republic, the competent authority is:

https://www.uoou.cz

We retain no Personal Data on Our own systems.
